# Design, Simulation, and Experimental Validation of a Desktop Wind Tunnel System

## Project Overview

This project encompasses the complete design, simulation, and physical construction of a desktop wind tunnel system. The goal is to create an affordable, accessible platform for conducting aerodynamic testing and qualitative analysis of small-scale CAD-designed models without requiring expensive industrial machinery.

## Purpose & Problem Statement

### The Challenge
Traditional aerodynamic testing requires access to large, expensive wind tunnels and sophisticated instrumentation—resources that are often prohibitively costly and inaccessible to most engineering teams, startups, and educational institutions.

### Our Solution
We are developing a desktop wind tunnel system that:
- Provides a cost-effective alternative to industrial wind tunnels
- Enables rapid prototyping and iterative design validation 
- Allows engineers to perform qualitative aerodynamic analysis on CAD-designed models
- Bridges the gap between simulation and real-world experimental validation

## Key Objectives

1. **Design Phase**
   - Develop comprehensive CAD models of the wind tunnel structure and components
   - Optimize airflow design for uniform flow characteristics
   - Design test section for easy model mounting and visualization

2. **Simulation Phase**
   - Conduct Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) simulations to validate design assumptions
   - Predict airflow patterns and velocity profiles
   - Optimize geometry for performance and efficiency
   - Account for potential stresses and forces on the model during potential testing

3. **Construction Phase**
   - Build a functional, scaled physical prototype
   - Source and integrate components (fan, ducting, sensors, instrumentation)
   - Ensure structural integrity and operational safety

4. **Experimental Validation**
   - Perform qualitative aerodynamic testing on small-scale models
   - Collect experimental data for correlation with simulations
   - Validate design assumptions and performance metrics

## Target Applications

- **Aerospace & Automotive**: Testing scaled aerodynamic designs for vehicles, aircraft components, and drones
- **Product Design**: Optimizing shapes for drag reduction and performance
- **Educational Research**: Teaching aerodynamics principles with hands-on experimentation
- **Rapid Prototyping**: Quick iteration cycles for design validation before expensive testing

Eliminates the need for expensive industrial wind tunnel facilities, reducing testing costs by orders of magnitude.

Makes aerodynamic testing accessible to teams of any size—from startups to established companies.

### Speed to Market
Enables rapid design iterations and quick feedback cycles during the development phase.

### Educational & Research Value
Provides hands-on learning opportunities and bridges simulation-to-reality validation gaps.

### Scalability
Demonstrates feasibility of desktop testing systems with potential for future enhancement and scaling.

## Project Scope

### Included
- Complete CAD design of wind tunnel components
- CFD simulation and analysis

### Out of Scope (Future Phases)
